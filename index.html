About Me

Hello! I am Wen Cong, a dedicated environmental engineer specializing in advanced water treatment technologies. My expertise spans from research to consulting, solving real-world water challenges with innovative solutions.

I have 7 years experience performeing studies on Coxsackievirus, Human Adenovirus, and Bacteriophage PR772, about their inactivation kinetics by chlorine and UV, inactivation mechanisms with quantitative PCR as a tool, and viral sensor development.

Since joining Trussell Technologies, Inc. in December 2022, I have been gaining hands-on design expeirence with water and wastewater treatment processes. Based in southern California, I've been deeply involved in water reuse projects, such as Direct Potable Reuse Research Project of Pure Water Southern California for Metropolitan Water District.
